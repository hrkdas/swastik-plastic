This page explains how cookies and similar technologies are used on swastikplastic.co.in.

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to make websites work, remember your preferences, and provide usage information.

Cookies we use

  • Essential — required for the site to function (e.g. remembering you accepted this notice).
  • Analytics — anonymous usage data to help us improve the site (only if enabled).
  • Embedded content — Google Maps may set cookies when you load the map on the contact page.

Managing cookies

Most browsers let you control cookies through settings. Disabling cookies may affect the site's functionality.
