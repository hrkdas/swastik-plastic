About Swastik PlasticTwo decades of mould-making muscle — built in Mumbai.

Founded in 2005, Swastik Plastic is a leading manufacturer of injection moulds and engineering plastic products. We serve industrial, medical, electrical and automotive customers with a focus on tight tolerances, repeatable quality and on-time delivery.

We specialise in Polycarbonate, Nylon 6, Nylon 66 and ABS — and run a wide polymer book covering commodity and engineering grades.

Our Vision

Manufacture moulds and industrial parts that are outstanding in innovation and quality — the most practical path consistent with our core values.

Our Mission

Use the latest moulding technology to deliver top-quality products at fair, reasonable cost — every batch, every time.

Injection Moulding

Vertical Moulding
